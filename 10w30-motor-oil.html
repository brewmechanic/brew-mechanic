American Stout

ABV

Tasting Notes

Intensely hopped stout with massive roast character - thick, dark, and viscous. Opaque black with dense tan head. Bold coffee and dark chocolate with resinous pine and citrus from aggressive hopping. Sharp espresso bitterness, smooth cocoa, and balancing caramel sweetness. Silky, full body from oats. Assertive West Coast-style bitterness complements the roast. Medium-full to full body.

Malts

  • Gladfield Medium Crystal Malt
  • Gladfield Munich Malt
  • Flaked Oats
  • Gladfield Roast Barley
  • Gladfield Dark Chocolate Malt

Hops

  • Columbus (Tomahawk)
  • Magnum
  • Centennial
  • Chinook

Yeast

Fermentis Safale US-05
