Tasting Notes

Sessionable tropical twist on the classic English mild. Deep amber-brown with ruby highlights. Toasted coconut, subtle chocolate, caramel, and nutty malt. Coconut complements rather than dominates. Sweet malt with toasted coconut, milk chocolate, toffee, and dark fruit. Low bitterness, smooth and easy-drinking. Light to medium body with soft, creamy mouthfeel. Perfect for long sessions.
