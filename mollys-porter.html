American Porter

ABV
6%

Tasting Notes

Robust porter with complex dark malt character. Opaque dark brown with ruby highlights. Rich chocolate, coffee, and toasted bread with hints of toffee and dark fruit. Smooth dark chocolate, subtle coffee roast, caramel sweetness, and burnt sugar notes. Subtle citrus and floral hops with a whisper of pine. Medium-full body with creamy mouthfeel.

  • Gladfield Wheat Malt
  • Gladfield Dark Chocolate Malt
  • Gladfield Toffee Malt
  • Gladfield Eclipse Wheat

Hops

  • Magnum
  • Cascade
  • Simcoe

Yeast

Fermentis Safale US-05
