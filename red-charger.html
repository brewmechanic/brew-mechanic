Tasting Notes

Full-throttle strong ale pushing boundaries on flavor and strength. Deep amber-red color with excellent clarity. Massive tropical hop aroma from triple dry hopping. Rich caramel and toffee sweetness with bready complexity and silky body from oats. Intense tropical fruit, citrus, pine resin, and caramel toffee. Dangerously drinkable for the ABV.
