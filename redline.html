Tasting Notes

Aggressive hop-forward IPA with rich amber-red malt depth. Deep copper-red color. Tropical fruit, citrus, and pine resin from heavy dry hopping. Biscuity, toasted malt base with caramel sweetness and subtle toffee. Massive hop presence with assertive bitterness. Medium-full body.
