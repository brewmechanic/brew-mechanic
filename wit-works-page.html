Witbier

ABV
5%
IBU
13

Tasting Notes

Classic Belgian witbier with traditional character. Hazy pale straw with fluffy white head. Bright citrus orange zest and subtle coriander spice. Pillowy soft mouthfeel from wheat and oats. Light body with creamy texture and lively carbonation. Best consumed fresh.

Malts & Grains

  • Gladfield Pilsner Malt
  • Gladfield Wheat Malt
  • Flaked Oats
  • Gladfield Munich Malt

Hops & Spices

  • Saaz
  • Coriander Seed (crushed)
  • Sweet Orange Peel (fresh)

Yeast

Mangrove Jack's M21 Belgian Wit
